🎪 What Is The Freak Circus?

The Freak Circus is a psychological 18+ horror dating simulator and visual novel that has taken the indie community by storm. The Circus of Horrors has arrived in town, bringing with it twisted secrets and a deadly romance.

You play as a café worker whose life changes forever after a chance encounter. Led by Pierrot, the silent yandere, and Harlequin, his seductive rival, you are pulled into a dangerous competition where affection meets obsession.

On December 3, 2025, Day 2 dropped — bringing fresh story branches, new characters, and darker secrets lurking beneath the circus tents.
